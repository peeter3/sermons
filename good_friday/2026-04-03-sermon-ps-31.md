# FATHER, INTO YOUR HANDS I COMMIT MY SPIRIT!

Let us pray. *\[Prayer]*  Amen.

Belovèd in the Lord, on this evening where we remember the single most glorious event in the history of man, we find that we are directed toward something that some of us pay attention to every day — we are directed toward the psalms. Tonight we heard our Lord's Fourth Word from the Cross: **"My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?"**  (Matt. 27:46)

Who are those forsaken by God?

We can do no worse than our Lord Jesus Christ if we pray the psalms constantly.

**The peace of God, which passes all understanding, keep your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus** (Phil. 4:7). Amen.
